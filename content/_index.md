GSM @ EMF 2026
{.h2}

We're running a small 2G and 4G network this year at EMF! With one of our SIM cards, you'll be able to call and text your friends on our
camp phone system; you'll just need a phone that supports GSM in the 1800 MHz band and LTE in the 2300MHz band (most European phones do).

To get support and or talk to us about these services, join us in the [#emfcamp-lte IRC
channel](https://web.libera.chat/?channel=#emfcamp-lte) on [Libera Chat](https://libera.chat/) (ignore the LTE, maybe next time)
