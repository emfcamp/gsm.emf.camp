---
title: LTE
description: >
  Connecting to our LTE network
---

We are expecting to have an LTE network on site this year.

You will need a handset that supports Band 40 (2300MHz) - most phones support this.

To connect, you will need to make sure that you add the following apn: `internet` in your phone's settings.

More setup information will be coming soon.

We will have NAT on IPv4 but you will be given a public IPv6 address.
